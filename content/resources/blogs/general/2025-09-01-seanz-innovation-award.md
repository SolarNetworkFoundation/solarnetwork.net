SolarNetwork Foundation is thrilled to have won the Are Ake Innovation Award for the SolarNetwork
platform at the 2025 SEANZ Conference held in Auckland on 28-29 August!

<!--more-->

<img alt="SEANZ Are Ake Innovation Award 2025" src="/img/general/seanz-snf-winner-ia-2025.png" width="200">

We stand amongst an [inspring cohort](https://www.seanz.org.nz/awards_25) of people and organisations
working tirelessly on amazing projects all across the renewable energy space.

Thank you to all our partners who use the platform to create such amazing applications and services:
[Ecosuite](https://ecosuite.io/), [Revolve Energy](https://www.revolveenergy.co/), [EVisi
Group](https://www.evisi.co/), [Energize Ōtaki](https://www.energiseotaki.nz/), [Greenstage
NZ](https://greenstage.co.nz/) and all the data wizards that are exploring and leveraging our APIs
for efficient energy data management and data synthesis. Together with these innovators we welcome
more participants into the SolarNetwork ecosystem.

Thanks again to [SEANZ](https://www.seanz.org.nz/) - Sustainable Energy Association of New Zealand,
and thanks to [Daniel Tomlinson](https://www.linkedin.com/in/talltomlinson/) for representing
SolarNetwork Foundation at the conference.
